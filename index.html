Information

The Haukai Restaurant is a New Zealand restaurant inspired by flavors from Maori and Pakeha kai, blending together for a perfect experience. Customers will be captivated by the presentation of te ao Maori, karakia for blessing food. Guests can choose to dine indoors or out on our patio that is perfect all seasons of the year. We serve dinner from Wednesday to Sunday inclusive.
The Restaurant specialises in Maori kai, it was started by a couple who are keen on creating a business that would appeal to locals and tourist visiting the area, one of the main motivation is that people learn about New Zealand traditions.

The restaurant is located in:

1 Island Street Kerikeri,
Bay of Islands.
