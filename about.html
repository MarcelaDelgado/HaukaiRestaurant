About us

Haukai Restaurant specialises in Maori kai, it was started by a couple who are keen on creating a business that would appeal to locals and tourist visiting the area, one of the main motivation is that people learn about New Zealand traditions and experience te ao Maori with the use of karakia for blessing the food.
